# The Evolution of Automation

For years, **Robotic Process Automation (RPA)** has been the workhorse of enterprise efficiency. It was simple: record a macro, play it back. If the button moved 5 pixels to the right, the bot crashed.

But we are entering a new era.

## The Cognitive Shift

With the advent of multimodal LLMs, agents can now "see" the screen not as a DOM tree or pixel coordinates, but as a semantic interface.

> "The screen scraping era was a necessary bridge. The semantic interaction era is the destination."

### Why this matters

1.  **Resilience**: UI changes don't break the bot.
2.  **Complexity**: Bots can make decisions based on unstructured data.
3.  **Speed**: Development time is cut by 80%.

We are moving away from *scripting* actions to *describing* outcomes.
