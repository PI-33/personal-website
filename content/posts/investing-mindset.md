# Separating Signal from Noise

Volatility is the price of admission for high returns. If you want the upside of a 100x bagger, you have to be willing to sit through a 50% drawdown.

## The Coffee Can Portfolio

The concept is simple: buy high-quality assets, put them in a "coffee can" (figuratively), and forget about them for 10 years.

*   **Don't** check the price daily.
*   **Do** check the fundamental thesis annually.
*   **Don't** try to time the top.

### Automation in Investing

I use Python scripts to monitor **fundamental metrics** rather than price action. When revenue growth decelerates below 15%, I get an alert. Otherwise, silence.
